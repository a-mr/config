The numeric representation of true and false in LOGICAL expressions has changed
In Intel Fortran Compiler 8.0, even integer values are considered .FALSE. and odd values are considered .TRUE. Furthermore, the logical constant .TRUE. has the value -1 rather than 1 as in previous versions of Intel Fortran
